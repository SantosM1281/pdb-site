Welcome!

Welcome to our P.O.P website; The name P.O.P was inspired by the Holy Spirit to a group of young women seeking God's face for the young people in their community and surrounding areas. P.O.P stands for Preaching Out to People after a night of prophetic revelation from the Holy Spirit, where the young group of PBMI will multiply and go out to the nations as pioneers of the Gospel.

Vision:

Our vision as P.O.P ministry is to help young people in our communities, to help them draw near to God and the purpose He wants for their life's. Come as you are... No strings attached.

Mission:

Reach, Restore, Ready, Remit
